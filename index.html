Walking Routes in Leeds

This map was inspired by the Walks In Leeds Facebook group, where Leeds locals share walking routes that they have enjoyed and post pictures and information about them.

This website was created to show some of the routes that have been shared, so that anyone in Leeds can get outside and get walking.

Walking is free and one of the easiest ways to get active, lose weight and become healthier. These are just some of the many health benefits of walking, not to mention it can also be an opportunity to catch up with a friend!

Have a walk in mind but don't know how long it is?

Feel free to plan your own route by using the controls on the left side of the map.
